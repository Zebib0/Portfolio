A speech recognition mobile app for deaf people

People who are deaf or have a hearing impairment have a
communication barrier in their daily life. This problem is
more apparent in their interaction with non-deaf people.
Currently we are observing more deaf people being part of schools,
higher education, and working within an inclusive environment.
While it is an encouraging phenomenon, they are being provided
with little to no aid regarding the additional help they actually
need to make their interaction smooth.
It is made flutter as frontend and python as a backend program
using the amharic speech recognition model from speechbrain.
